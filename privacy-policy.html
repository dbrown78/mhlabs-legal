Privacy Policy

ODIN AutoAlert — Last updated: April 14, 2026

AutoAlert collects only what is necessary to provide vehicle diagnostics, predictive maintenance, and mechanic discovery. We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your data for advertising.

1. Information We Collect

Account information. Name, email, hashed password.

Vehicle & diagnostic data. Year, make, model, VIN; OBD2 DTCs; sensor readings (RPM, temp, fuel trim, O2 values); scan history.

Bluetooth data. Connection metadata stays in-session only, never transmitted to servers.

Location data. Used only for Mechanic Finder (while app is in use). Not stored on our servers.

Usage data. App version, OS version, anonymous crash reports.
